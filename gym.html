About

I began to go to the gym during university, using the state of the art gym included in my tuition at UCR. The SRC is the biggest gym I have been in and had a plethora amount of equipment to use. I started to get serious during the summer of my sophomore year, learning about training until failure and macros. The best time to go to the gym is early in the morning when there are less people around. The gym has become a part of my daily ritual.
