Josh Allen

The man who will take the bills to the superbowl

Time line of Josh Allen's Career:

  • 2018 - Drafted by the Buffalo Bills in the first round and seventh overall.
  • 2019 - Became a starter, becoming the head captain of the team. Clinched a playoff spot.
  • 2020 - Allen named AFC offensive player of the week four times. Named to the 2021 Pro Bowl.
  • 2021 - Face the Chiefs in the AFC Championship and lose 24-38.
  • 2022 - Face the Chiefs in the AFC Divisional round and lose 36-42.
  • 2023 - This year Josh Allen and the Buffalo Bills face the Cincinatti Bengals in the Divisional round and lose 27-10.
  • 2024 - Face the chiefs again in the AFC Divisional round and lose 24-27.
  • 2025 - This year the season looked strong and they faced the Kansas City Chiefs in the AFC Championship but with the refs help the Buffalo Bills lose again 29-32.
  • 2026 - To be continued.....
